GIRAFFES

Giraffes are the world's tallest mammals,they have towering legs and long necks. Giraffe's legs alone are taller than many humans about 6 feet, these long legs allows giraffes to run as fast as 35 miles an hour over short distance and cruise comfortably at 10 miles an hour over longer distances. Giraffe weight is 1,750 to 2,800 pounds. An adult male can grow to round 5.5m. Giraffesare found in the dry savannah of Africa.

Giraffes tower Africa's plains. Giraffes are indentified by their long necks equally long and spindly legs, and spotted coast, most giraffes have a tan, white or yellow coats that are spotted with brown, square shapes.

Giraffes are herbivore and can be found in different habitent in grasslands and open woodlands.
