MONKEYS

Monkeys are tree-dwelling (arboreal) simians. They are in the primate order they are intelligent, social animals. Most monkeys have a tail. Monkeys tend to inhabit the tropical rainforests of Africa, Central America and Asia.

A group of monkeys is called a troop. Most primates share six basic features: foward- facing eyes, eye sockets, grasping hands, nails, fingerprints and large brains.All monkeys has fur, but it can be lots of different colors, usually brown or black.

Monkeys are omnivores and are found in all primates trees.
