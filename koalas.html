KOALAS

Koala is an iconic Australian animal. Often called the koala "bear", this tree climbing animal is maesupial-a mammal with a pouch for the development of offspring. Koala lives in the eucalyptus forest of southeastern and eastern Australia. They rely on eucalyptus tree for both habitant and food.

Koala look fuzzy, their hair is more like the coarse wool of a sheep they have two oppositing thumbs on their hands, and both feet and hands have rough pads and claws to grab on to branches and they have two toes, fuesed together, on their feet, which they use to comb their fur

Koalas are herbivore and can be found in eucalyptus forest.
